On a mission to find a plumbus, the portal gun malfunctioned and you are trapped in an asteroid field. You must avoid the asteroids! Press the mouse button to ignite your thrusters! Use the arrow keys to navigate around the asteroids.

Oh! One more thing, A monstrous black hole is pulling you down, so don't forget to keep that thruster button pressed to avoid a squidgy death... or a splatty exploding death!
